ENERGY, EMISSION and WASTE
All connected to climate change, the biggest threat to humanity

This project visualizes te connection between energy production, energy use, carbon emissions and generation of waste for European countries.

For everything in our daily life we need energy. A variety of energy sources makes our economy spin and keeps the world population alive. In the past decades we have gained both frightening and valuable knowledge about our relation with the energy sources that we have and that we use. Currently we emit a huge amount of carbon dioxide into the atmosphere and produce complete islands of waste by the way we produce and use energy.

We leave behind an indelible mark on planet earth and climate change is a major thread to sustainable development.
Luckily the use of renewable energy is rising and we are fighting to bring down carbon emissions. How are the countries of Europe dealing with their energy use and hazardous output? Scroll down and compare them all.

Big consumers, big polluters?

Find out how production and use of energy, CO2 emission and generation of municipal waste are connected in European countries. Every line and every dot represent a country. Only interested in countries that use particularly little energy per capita? Select a low range on the energy-ax in the left graph and find out how those countries score on the other variables. Look at Iceland's skyrocketing values! For more information about the outliers, look here.

In the scatterplot emphasis is put on the use of renewable energy. A trend becomes visible: the higher the use of renewable energy per capita, the lower the CO2 emissions per capita. Hover over the elements in the graphs and the corresponding line or dot in the other graph will be highlighted. Some countries are only visible in one of the graphs due to missing data. One country that catches your attention? C L I C K on the line/dot and discover more...


Slide through the years and watch for example Luxembourg's and Iceland's energy use skyrocketing.

Energy Use

The energy use of a country is specified for every energy source. Renewable energy, showed in green, is causing the least harm to our environment. How is the country of your interest scoring?

Emission

A country's carbon emission is specified by the emission of several economic sectors. Overall these sectors are the most polluting economic sectors.

Hazardous Waste

Hazardous waste is waste that poses substantial or potential threats to public health or the environment. Again, the production is showed per economic sector.
